Spreadsheet¶

  • Link Odoo data
  • Functions
  • Templates

Spreadsheet lets you organize, analyze, and visualize your data in tabular form. Among others, you can:

  • Insert and link your Odoo data (pivots, graphs, lists, and menus).

  • Use formulas and functions.

  • Use default templates or create new ones.

  • Format data.

  • Sort and filter data.
