===================================================
Portal
===================================================

To access the MyLabs portal it is necessary to sign in with a username and password.
`Click here <https://mylabs.es/web/login>`_ to access the login form.

.. image:: portal/home_page.png
   :align: center
   :alt: Home page

.. image:: portal/sign_in.png
   :align: center
   :alt: Sign in form

.. note::
   If you do not have these details, contact the MyLabs team to request your credentials through
   the **Contact Us** form. `Click here <https://mylabs.es/contactus>`_ to access the form.

The portal is the main page of the MyLabs customer area. It is the first page that appears when
you log in. It contains the main following sections:

- **Details**: It contains the user's personal data. Click :guilabel:`Edit` to modify the data.
- **Account security**: It contains the option to change the password.

- **Customer**: It contains the documents in which the user is the customer and orders services.
- **Vendor**: It contains the documents in which the user is the vendor and provide services.

.. image:: portal/portal.png
   :align: center
   :alt: MyLabs Portal

.. note::
   The red circles indicate the unviewed documents in the section.
