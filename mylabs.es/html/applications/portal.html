Portal¶

To access the MyLabs portal it is necessary to sign in with a username and password. Click here to access the login form.

Note

If you do not have these details, contact the MyLabs team to request your credentials through the Contact Us form. Click here to access the form.

The portal is the main page of the MyLabs customer area. It is the first page that appears when you log in. It contains the main following sections:

  • Details: It contains the user’s personal data. Click Edit to modify the data.

  • Account security: It contains the option to change the password.

  • Customer: It contains the documents in which the user is the customer and orders services.

  • Vendor: It contains the documents in which the user is the vendor and provide services.

Note

The red circles indicate the unviewed documents in the section.
